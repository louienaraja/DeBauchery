OUR PHILOSOPY

We are the one-stop shop for all your meaty needs. We pride ourselves in treating butchery as an art, knowing every move and part by heart. It is our mission to continually explore and develop all the butchery possibilities for you.

De*Bauchery aims to provide all customers clean cut and bang for the buck meat, whether it be beef, pork, or poultry. All butchers are trained experts who pledge to make you feel like bringing home the bacon. Our top-grade products come from organically-fed farm animals from New Zealand, so there's no risk of any antibiotics.

De*Bautchery is your place to go for convienient, clean, and secure meat shopping needs. All of those for the honest and right price. Come and visit us. We'll be waiting for you.
